About Me

Welcome. I have always had a passion for helping people and for working with computers. My passion for people led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from The Ohio State University. After graduation, I developed an interest in coding and ended up completing courses in Software Development. Most recently, I joined the We Can Code IT coding bootcamp. The coding languages that the program focuses on are Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am excited to start a career in Software Development.
